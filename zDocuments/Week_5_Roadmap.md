# Indipips - Week 5 Roadmap: Launch & Growth Operations

We have built a fortress. Now, we open the gates. Week 5 is about moving to the cloud, inviting our first traders, and building the viral loops that will scale Indipips to 10k users.

## 🚀 Phase 6: Cloud Deployment & Scaling (Day 23-24)
- **Objective**: Moving from local development to a globally distributed cloud environment.
- **Tasks**:
  - AWS/Railway Production Deployment (Dockerized).
  - PostgreSQL & Redis Managed Instance clustering.
  - SSL & Custom Domain (`indipips.com`) configuration.
  - Auto-scaling rules for handling volume spikes.

## 🤝 Phase 7: The Viral Loop (Affiliate System) (Day 25-26)
- **Objective**: Incentivizing traders to bring other traders.
- **Tasks**:
  - Implement a multi-tier Referral System.
  - Automated Commission tracking for influencers/affiliates.
  - Affiliate Dashboard (Link generation, Payout tracking).

## 📊 Phase 8: Advanced Monitoring & Support (Day 27-28)
- **Objective**: Ensuring 99.9% uptime and institutional-level support.
- **Tasks**:
  - **Sentry/LogRocket**: Real-time error tracking and session replay.
  - **Intercom/Zendesk Integration**: Live chat support for traders.
  - **Automated Email Engine (SendGrid)**: Welcome series, Breach alerts, and Payout confirmations.

## 🏁 Phase 9: Soft Launch (Day 29+)
- **Objective**: Verifying the system with a select group of beta traders.
- **Tasks**:
  - "Early Access" invite-only rollout.
  - Real-money feedback loop.
  - Final UI/UX polish based on beta data.

# Indipips Bespoke Design Refinement

The goal is to move from a FundingPips "clone" to a unique "Indipips" brand identity while maintaining the premium, trustable feel.

## Proposed Design Shifts

### 🎨 Color Palette (Indipips Signature)
- **Primary**: `Royal Indigo (#4338ca)` - Deeper and more professional than standard Indigo.
- **Accent**: `Mint Growth (#10b981 / #34d399)` - Representing prosperity and growth.
- **Background**: `Slate Soft (#f8fafc)` with subtle `Indigo-White` gradients.

### 🍱 UI Components
- **Sidebar**: Instead of just a narrow white strip, we'll use a **"Glass-Indigo"** sidebar for a more modern, tech-forward look.
- **Widgets**: Move from plain boxes to **Soft-Glow** cards.
- **Account Selection**: Redesign as a "Floating Account Orb" or a more integrated navigation element rather than a standard sidebar.

### 🇮🇳 Indian Market Context
- **Currency Support**: Add UI placeholders for **INR (₹)** conversion/display options.
- **Session Indicators**: Explicitly highlight the **NSE/BSE (Indian Market)** session hours alongside Global sessions.
